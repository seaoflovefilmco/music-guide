Before we get to the fun stuff

Let's talk about music for your video

We know you already have a Spotify playlist full of songs you love. Here's why we can't just use those tracks in your wedding film, and what we can do instead.

This applies to every wedding videographer, not just us
Even if a videographer says they can use any song, they cannot legally do so. Using unlicensed commercial music exposes you and them to copyright strikes, video takedowns, and in serious cases, financial penalties.
🎵

Songs are intellectual property

Artists and labels own their music. Using it commercially, even in a personal video shared online, requires a sync licence.

📹

Your video will be taken down

Platforms like YouTube, Instagram and Vimeo automatically detect unlicensed audio and will mute or remove your video.

💸

Sync licences are expensive

Licensing a single popular song for video use typically costs $500–$5,000+. It's not a simple fee we can add on.

✅

Licensed music sounds great

We use curated libraries with thousands of professional tracks across every genre and mood. You will find something you love.

Understanding your options

Commercial music vs licensed music

The difference is simple. Commercial music is made to be sold and streamed. Licensed music is made specifically for use in video, with the rights already cleared.
